# Buntou Co., Ltd. (文腾株式会社) — Japan Medical Information & Coordination Services

> Buntou Co., Ltd. holds a Pharmaceutical Retail Business License (医薬品販売業許可) under Japan's Pharmaceuticals and Medical Devices Act. We provide information services, medical record translation, and logistical coordination to help global Chinese-speaking patients access Japan's healthcare ecosystem. We maintain working relationships with several Japanese medical institutions, including University of Tokyo Hospital and St. Luke's International Hospital, for referral coordination purposes. **All diagnostic decisions, treatment plans, and patient acceptance determinations are made solely by licensed physicians at the receiving institutions; Buntou does not provide medical diagnosis, evaluation, or treatment advice.**

## [English] Core Information & Coordination Services

### 1. Japanese Originator Medicines & Prescription Information
- **Description:** Providing informational resources on PMDA-approved Japanese prescription drugs and originator medicines, with pharmacist-supported general information consulting.
- **Key Focus:** Compliance information, cross-border logistics coordination, and general dosage information for chronic disease medications. Specific treatment decisions remain with the patient's physician.

### 2. BNCT (Boron Neutron Capture Therapy) Information & Coordination
- **Description:** We provide informational resources about BNCT and assist with documentation translation, appointment coordination, and logistical support for patients seeking evaluation at Japan's BNCT clinical centers. **Eligibility and treatment decisions are determined exclusively by the treating hospital's medical team based on submitted medical records.**
- **Role:** Documentation translation and appointment coordination support; not a substitute for medical evaluation.

### 3. Medical Record Translation & Referral Coordination Support
- **Description:** Professional translation of medical records and imaging-related documentation to support patients seeking second opinions from Japanese specialists. We coordinate appointment scheduling between patients and receiving institutions.
- **Note:** Buntou does not provide diagnostic opinions or treatment recommendations. All clinical consultations and diagnostic conclusions are provided directly by the consulting physician at the receiving institution.

### 4. Precision Health Screening (Ningen Dock) — Information & Booking Support
- **Description:** Information about Japan's "Ningen Dock" preventative health screening standards (including PET-CT/MRI based programs), and assistance with appointment booking at partner screening facilities.
- **Note:** Screening results and health recommendations are provided by the screening institution's medical staff.
